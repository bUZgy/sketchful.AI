About the Game

Sketchful AI is a game inspired by Google's QuickDraw and Sketchful.io . The goal for this game is to inspire players to learn about the world of AI. This game shows players that AI isn't just for cars and statistics but is easily accessible for video games as well.

This game was made by just a few high school students wanting to learn more about AI; proof that high quality products are attainable with such young minds.

Try it out!

Timeline

Week 1

Our project had to be Computer Vision related meaning the AI had to take a type of image input and output a result. Our ideas were ranging from plant identification to hand gesture Flappy Bird but an unanimous vote chose Sketchful AI. We began planning how to implement our plan into a real game.

Week 2

We worked on gathering data for our project through Google Quickdraw. Google Quickdraw contained a variety of classes of images, that we could use to implement our Sketchful AI game. We gathered our data and uploaded it onto Roboflow, where we proceeded to tweak our model until it was able to identify the images supplied by Google Quickdraw's plentiful collection. Quickdraw's data actually did not only contain images, but the individual keystrokes of every user.

Week 3

We began building this website to house our game. We picked out various colors, designs, and layouts for our website. We also created a video to advertise our version of Sketchful AI. We added more classes to our model, and continued training/testing it.

About the Model

A confusion matrix presents a table layout of the different outcomes of the prediction and results of a classification problem and helps visualize its outcomes. It plots a table of all the predicted and actual values of a classifier. Our model presents the true labels of the sketches on the y-axis and the predicted label that our model generated on the x-axis. As evinced by the sea of black surrounding thr diagonal in the center, our model is pretty acurate (99.4% accuracy with our training data and validation data).
